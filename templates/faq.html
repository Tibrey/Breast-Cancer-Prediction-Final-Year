Breast Cancer

The Frequently Asked Question

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that forms in the cells of the breast. It is one of the most common cancers affecting women worldwide, although it can also occur in men. Breast cancer occurs when abnormal cells in the breast grow uncontrollably, forming a tumor. These cancerous cells can invade nearby tissues and spread to other parts of the body through the bloodstream or lymphatic system.
